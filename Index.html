ImageShare
A cloud hosted image sharing web application developed with .NET Core MVC.

ImageShare is a Cloud hosted web applicatin that I developed to store and share images/photos and also to share with other users. ImageShare has an authorization policy which means that in order to use the webApp, the person must be registered and loged in. Once the user is loged in they can utilise any of the features on the webApp.

Fetures of ImageShare
  • View their own photos
  • Upload a new photo and create its metadata
  • Delete a photo and its metadata
  • View the details of the photos and their metadata
  • Edit/updata the details of the photos and their metadata
  • Share the photo with other users
  • Search for a photo by its metadata
  • Download photos

GymScore
GymScore is a software package developed with C# and sqlServer

GymScore is a gymnastics competition management system developed with C# and sqlServer. One of my first major solo projects, GymScore was challaning to develop due to the vast amount of advanced sql querys it utilise. GymScore still contains some fo the most qomplex sql queries that I have created to date.

GymScore Features
  • GymScore can generate a .xlsx entry form to send out with competition invetations to prospective competetors
  • The .xlsx can be used to automatically enter mass amounts of gymnasts into the database
  • Or gymnasts can be entered individualy to accomodate late entries
  • GymScore can then generate competition groups and print out all the scoresheets needed in the competition
  • The scores are entered into a dynamic dataview that updates the database as you type
  • Final scores and rankings are calculated by GymScore and can be printed
